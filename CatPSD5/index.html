About Hawkins-Hamilton

The Hawkins-Hamilton Company was incorporated as a Manufacturer’s

Representative in 1914 and has been in continuous operation for

over 100 years. We supply equipment to the Power, Process, and

Shipbuilding Industries in Virginia, the Carolinas, and Maryland.

Our goal is to offer the best product at a fair price while

subscribing to the highest level of business and personal ethics.

Our staff and the manufacturers we represent are dedicated to

providing unparalleled customer service. We can help you select

the best equipment to meet your requirements and we welcome an

opportunity to become an integral part of your supply chain.
